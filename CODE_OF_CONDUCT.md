# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Use direct, respectful, technically grounded collaboration. Prioritize clarity,
reviewability, and safety over cleverness.

## Expected Behavior

- explain tradeoffs concretely
- keep review comments actionable
- avoid hostile, dismissive, or intentionally vague communication
- surface security and operational risks early

## Enforcement

The repository owner may remove content or restrict participation when behavior
undermines safe collaboration.
